Overview

ParentHub is designed to help families manage shared information. This policy explains what information may be collected, how it may be used, and how users can request help.

Information collected

ParentHub may collect information that users provide directly in the app, such as account details, family details, notes, preferences, and support requests.

How information is used

Information may be used to provide app features, maintain accounts, respond to support requests, improve reliability, and protect the app from misuse.

Sharing

Personal information is not sold. Information may be shared with service providers when needed to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, or protect user safety and app security.
