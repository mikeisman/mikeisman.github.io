Weightroom

Support

Weightroom is a minimalist lifting companion for building workouts, running active sessions, tracking rest, and reviewing workout history on iPhone, with an Apple Watch companion for active sessions.

How To Get Help

Email support with a short description of the issue, what you expected to happen, and what happened instead. Screenshots are helpful when the issue is visual.

Contact

Support is handled by email for this release.

weightroomapp@isman.com

Response Expectations

This is a small independent app, so response times may vary. Clear repro steps make it easier to help quickly.

What Weightroom Covers

The current release focuses on workout templates, active workout sessions, local workout history, and Apple Watch companion controls for active sessions.

Data Handling

Weightroom currently stores workout data locally on your device in this first release. For more detail, see the Privacy Policy.
