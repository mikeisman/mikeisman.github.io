Weightroom

Privacy Policy

Weightroom is designed to keep the first release simple. The app currently stores workout information locally on your device and does not require an account to use core features.

What The App Stores

Weightroom stores workout templates, active-session state, and completed workout history on your device so the app can run your workouts and show past sessions.

What The App Does Not Collect

This first release does not provide an account system, cloud sync, advertising profiles, or social features. It is not intended to collect personal data for tracking or marketing.

Third-Party Sharing

Weightroom does not intentionally share your workout data with third parties in this first release.

Apple Watch Companion

The Apple Watch app mirrors and controls an active iPhone workout session. It relies on the paired iPhone session rather than a separate cloud account or remote profile.
